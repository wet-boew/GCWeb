Grouping tabular cells with the thead, tbody, tfoot, colgroup and col elements

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet Proin pellentesque sodales cursus
Nunc dictum felis at dignissim dictum Curabitur ut leo nisi
Felis dictum nunc at dictum dignissim Leo ut curabitur nisi 
<table>
<colgroup>
<col span="1">
</colgroup>
<thead>
	<tr>
		<th>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet</th>
		<th>Proin pellentesque sodales cursus</th>
	</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
	<tr>
		<td>Nunc dictum felis at dignissim dictum</td>
		<td>Curabitur ut leo nisi</td>
	</tr>
</tbody>
<tfoot>
	<tr>
		<td>Felis dictum nunc at dictum dignissim</td>
		<td>Leo ut curabitur nisi</td>
	</tr>
</tfoot>
</table>
