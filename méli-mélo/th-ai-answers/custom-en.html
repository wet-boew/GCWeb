This page serves as an example of for AI answers beta testing banner and rescue link with custom content.

To customize the text content of the AI Answers banner and rescue link, you can add the following attributes within the ai-answers-trigger container:

  • data-banner-title for the banner title
  • data-banner-link-text for the banner link text
  • data-rescue-title for the rescue title
  • data-rescue-link-text for the rescue link text

Notes

  • Make sure to escape HTML entities in the attribute values, such as using &lt; for < and &gt; for >.
  • Every attribute is optional.
  • To remove the title before the link, simply add the the attribute with an empty value.

Instructions

Insert the following code snippet in your page to activate the AI Answers banner.

<div class="ai-answers-trigger"
	data-banner-title=""
	data-banner-link-text="Ask AI Answers for assistance"
	data-rescue-title="&lt;strong&gt;Need help?&lt;/strong&gt;"
	data-rescue-link-text="Ask AI Answers for help"></div>
