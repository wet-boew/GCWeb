By setting the noFooterAbout and noFooterSite variables to "true", the 'About gvernment' section and all utility links from 'About this site' will be hidden on page load but the Canada wordmark will remain.

Also by hidding all footers, the "Skip to About this site" is disabled from the skip navigation links.
