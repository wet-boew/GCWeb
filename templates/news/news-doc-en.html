News landing page template - Version 4.0

List of changes

  • Version 4.0:
    • Changed paragraph above news items.
    • Changed paragraph below news items.
  • Version 3.0:
    • Removal of the promotions section at the top of the page.
    • Addition of introduction text to the left of the Follow section.
    • Addition of randomized functionality for the three items in the features section.
    • Update of the advanced search link URL.

Transition from version 3.0 to 4.0

  • Change paragraph above news items from:
    <p>To access all articles, please visit the <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/news/advanced-news-search/news-results.html">advanced search</a>.</p>
    to:
    <p class="nojs-hide"><a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/news/advanced-news-search.html">Perform an advanced search</a></p>
  • Change paragraph below news items from:
    <p>To access all articles, please visit the <a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/news/advanced-news-search/news-results.html">advanced search</a>.</p>
    to:
    <p class="text-center nojs-hide"><a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/news/advanced-news-search/news-results.html">Access all news products</a></p>
  • Add the following block right after the first paragraph (before the news products table): 
    <div class="nojs-show"><p>News products are not available in basic HTML view. You can either:</p>
			<ul>
				<li><a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/news/advanced-news-search/news-results.html">Access all news products</a> or</li>
				<li><a href="https://www.canada.ca/en/news/advanced-news-search.html">Perform an advanced search</a></li>
			</ul>
		</div>
